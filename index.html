About Cafe Oktober

Cafe Oktober is a vibrant, modern cafe and restaurant located in North Bongaigaon, Assam. Known for its cool décor, lively vibe, and a menu that mixes the best of continental and local flavors, it's a top pick for hangouts, group parties, or a cozy date.

Address Chapaguri Road, near Agarwala Hospital, North Bongaigaon, Bongaigaon, Assam 783380
Opening Hours 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM (Monday to Sunday)
Special Features Live Music, Cozy Ambiance, Photo-friendly interiors, Takeaway, Delivery
